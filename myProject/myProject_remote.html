Astronaut Rescue: Remote Interface

An astronaut has been lost in space and we need you to retrieve her. Use the space ship to navigate the astroid fields, pick up the astronaut, and bring her to the safe zone. To control the spaceship click the different buttons to add instructions to the queue. Once you press run the list of instructions will run as you ordred it.
